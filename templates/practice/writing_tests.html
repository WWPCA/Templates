IELTS Writing Tips

Task 1 Tips

  • Spend about 20 minutes on this task
  • Include an introduction, overview, and 2-3 detailed paragraphs
  • Describe key trends, don't explain reasons
  • Use appropriate data vocabulary (increase, decrease, fluctuate)
  • Use a variety of sentence structures

Task 2 Tips

  • Spend about 40 minutes on this task
  • Include an introduction, 2-3 body paragraphs, and conclusion
  • Fully address all parts of the question
  • Support your ideas with examples and reasoning
  • Use paragraphs effectively to organize ideas
  • Use a range of vocabulary and grammatical structures
