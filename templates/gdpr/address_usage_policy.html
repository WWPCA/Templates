Customer Address Usage Policy

This policy explains how IELTS AI Prep collects and uses customer address information for tax compliance purposes.

Why We Collect Address Information

IELTS AI Prep collects customer billing and shipping address information during the payment process for the following purposes:

  • To comply with tax laws and regulations in various jurisdictions
  • To determine the correct tax rate for your purchase based on your location
  • To provide required information to tax authorities when necessary
  • To verify eligibility for our services based on your country of residence

How Address Information is Collected

We collect address information through our secure payment provider, Stripe. When you make a purchase:

  • You will be asked to provide your billing address
  • Address information is transmitted securely via encrypted connections
  • Stripe securely processes this information for payment verification
  • We receive this information securely through Stripe's webhook system

How We Store Address Information

Your address information is:

  • Stored securely in our database with encryption
  • Associated with your payment records
  • Retained for the duration required by applicable tax laws (typically 7 years)
  • Accessible only to authorized personnel for tax compliance purposes

Your Rights Regarding Address Information

As part of your personal data, you have the following rights regarding your address information:

  • The right to access your address information through our data export feature
  • The right to correct inaccurate address information
  • The right to request deletion of your address information (subject to tax retention requirements)

Please note that we may be legally required to retain address information for completed transactions even after account deletion, to comply with tax requirements.

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your address information, including:

  • Database encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure transmission using TLS/SSL encryption
  • Strict access controls limiting who can view address information
  • Regular security audits and assessments

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes.
