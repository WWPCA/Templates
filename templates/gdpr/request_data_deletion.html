Important Information

Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please review the options carefully before proceeding.

Deletion Options

We will delete your sensitive data while maintaining your basic account. This includes:

  • All transcripts of speaking tests
  • Detailed assessment results
  • Test history and performance data

Your account will remain active, but your test history and results will be removed. You can continue using your account with a fresh start.

We will permanently delete your entire account and all associated data. This includes:

  • Your account information
  • All test results and assessments
  • Subscription and payment history
  • All activity and usage data

Warning: This action cannot be undone. Your account will be permanently deleted.

Security Verification

For your security, we'll send a verification code to complete this request. This helps ensure that only you can delete your personal data.

What Happens After Deletion

Partial Deletion

  • Your account remains active
  • You retain access to the platform
  • Your subscription status is preserved
  • All test results and sensitive data are removed

Complete Deletion

  • Your account is permanently deleted
  • You lose access to all platform features
  • Your subscription is canceled
  • You'll need to register again to use the service

Note: Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes, such as transaction records required by financial regulations.
